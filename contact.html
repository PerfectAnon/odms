Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my tax receipt?

A PDF receipt is emailed automatically within minutes of a successful payment. You can also download it from your donor dashboard.

How do I verify an NGO?

Every NGO on HeartGive is verified before listing. You can also search any Receipt ID on our Transparency Dashboard to confirm a transaction.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. During donation, simply check the "Donate Anonymously" option. Your name will not appear publicly, but you'll still receive your receipt.

How do NGOs register?

Use the contact form above, select "NGO Registration" and include your registration number. Our team will reach out within 2 business days.
